Board Menu
* select board action: scroll or number keys
* execute board action: V or left click
* select root board: hold ctrl
* delete non-empty board: move, then press T

Board Placing
* mirror board: m or n (board must have no child boards)
* toggle flat/standing: right click
* rotate board: QEZX
* offset placement: scroll/CTRL + scroll
* place board: left click
* destroy board: esc or T

Color Board
* change color: scroll
* apply color: V or left click
* cancel: esc

New Board

* confirm: space or V

Text Edit Menu
* change text size: scroll
* done: esc

Choose Display Color

Noisemaker Menu
* select note: number keys or scroll
* change octave: ctrl + scroll
* confirm: space or enter
